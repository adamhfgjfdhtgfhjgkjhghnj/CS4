Legal, Ethical & Privacy Issues in Computing

Understanding the real impact of technology on society

Legal Issues & Intellectual Property

Copyright laws and software licenses protect creators but can also limit innovation by restricting access to tools and content.

Privacy Concerns

Data is collected automatically through apps and websites, often without users fully realizing it. This raises concerns about control and security.

Social & Economic Impact

Data improves services but can lead to surveillance and misuse. Companies benefit economically while users may lose privacy.

Environmental Impact

Computing increases e waste and energy use, affecting the environment globally.

Security & Protection

Malware like phishing and ransomware can damage systems. Strong passwords, encryption, and updates help protect data.
